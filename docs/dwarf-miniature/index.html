Painting guide — novice
  • Prime grey — shows all surface detail
  • Basecoat skin with Bugman's Glow
  • Armour: Macragge Blue + Agrax wash
  • Axe blade: Leadbelcher + Nuln Oil
  • Beard: Zandri Dust + Seraphim Sepia
  • Belt leather: Rhinox Hide + dry-brush Doombull
  • Bald head: highlight with Kislev Flesh
  • Base: texture paste + Agrax, dry-brush cream
